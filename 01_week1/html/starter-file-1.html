What is Semantics?

Posted on June 4, 2025

Semantics is the branch of linguistics and logic concerned with meaning. It focuses on the relationship between signifiers—like words, phrases, signs—and what they stand for.

Did You Know?

The word “semantics” comes from the Greek word semantikos, meaning “significant.” It was first used in the late 19th century to refer to the science of meaning in language.
